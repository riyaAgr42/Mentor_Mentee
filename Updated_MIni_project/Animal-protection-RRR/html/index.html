About Our Mission

At Save Animals Now, we believe every injured animal deserves a second chance. Our mission is to connect people who find injured animals with NGOs specializing in rescue and rehabilitation. We ensure quick action to transport animals to veterinary hospitals for immediate care and provide ongoing support throughout their recovery.

Together, we aim to create a safer world for animals and inspire people to join hands in this noble cause.
